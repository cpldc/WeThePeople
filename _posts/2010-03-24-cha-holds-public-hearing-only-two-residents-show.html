The Chicago Housing Authority held a public hearing on March 22, 2010 at the Charles A. Hayes Family Investment Center, for the public to comment on their proposals to make amendments to their Moving To Work (MTW) Agreement, which they established with the U. S. Department of Housing and Urban Development.
But few residents showed up to comment. Click here to see Residents' Journal's video coverage of the event.
