The Jewish Council on Urban Affairs, a social justice group, which has been tracking and grading the progress of the City of Chicago's' public services since 2007, launched a new website on March 17, 2010 called Developing Government Accountability to the People or (DGAP).
The new website is designed for the public at large to use to evaluate the performance of Mayor Richard M. Daley, and their Aldermen's policies, practices and efforts concerning housing, education, economic development, transportation, the environment, criminal justice, and ethics and corruption.
