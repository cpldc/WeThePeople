Support

The Urban Youth International Journalism Program is directed by We The People Media, a 501 c3 not-for-profit organization recognized by the federal Internal Revenue Service as well as the State of Illinois. We The People Media receives support from advertisers and subscribers as well as individual donors and charitable foundations.

A donation to We The People Media for the Urban Youth International Journalism Program will be used to pay instructors for the young people, provide the students with payments for their articles and other costs.
