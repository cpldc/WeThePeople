Residents' Journal is published four times annually and is distributed free to all Chicago public housing residents and participants in the Section 8 subsidized housing program. If you are not a public housing resident and would like to support Residents' Journal's plan to become a financially self-sufficient entity you may purchase a print subscription.

A personal subscription costs $25 per year. Sponsor subscribers, whose names appear on page 3 of each issue, pay just $100 per year.
