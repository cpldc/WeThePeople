All donations to We The People Media are tax-deductible. If you would like to support our work, please make an on-line donation:

Any amount is appreciated, but a donation of $100 or more or more automatically places you on the list of subscribers to the print edition of Residents’ Journal for one year, and ensures that your name will be printed in a special section of the publication. An annual donation of $25 or more places you on the list of subscribers to the print edition of Residents’ Journal for one year.

We The People Media is a 501 c3 not-for-profit organization recognized by the federal Internal Revenue Service as well as the State of Illinois. We The People Media receives support from advertisers and subscribers as well as individual donors and charitable foundations.
